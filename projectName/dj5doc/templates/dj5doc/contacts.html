Contact Us

A contact us page is a key part of a website. It allows visitors to contact a business without leaving the browser. It can also help with customer service and lead generation.



A contact us page should include:

Contact information:
Phone number, email address, and other contact information
Contact form:
A short form with fields to help identify the contact
Introduction:
Phone number, email address, and other contact information
Social media:
Links to social media channels


A contact us page can also:

  • Explain why someone should contact the business
  • Describe how the business can help
  • Provide guidance for existing customers
  • Offer an overview of the brand for new visitors


Here are some ways to say "contact us":

  • Drop us a line
  • Get in touch
  • Reach out
  • Let's chat
  • Talk to us
  • Get ahold of us
